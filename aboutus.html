~About Us~

PROLOGUE

The Lost Chapters of Worlds Bookstore is an archive where a maze of stories come together and be stored for all the Book lovers to oversee the different worlds and their histories of time. It is a place bustling with Laughter of Happiness, Cries of Sadness, Hums of Curiosity and Fumes of Anger, a place heavily influenced by the emotions of the ‘Characters’ making the Book lovers connect with them on a more personal and emotional level and falling in love with the ‘Characters’.

Each Chapter, Each Page, Each Sentence and Each Words brings about a story of a ‘Characters’ life. Their happy moments, sweet moments, sad moments, pain and struggles and their victory to the Book Lovers. ‘Characters’ needs Book Lovers, for if their world isn’t watched by Book Lovers, time stops like a forgotten history for the ‘Characters’, making them live in the same state, unmoving, forever more. Becoming a destroyed and forgotten world, with no sign of life.

Our Team

Izyan

Queen, as the ruler of the Lost Chapter of the World Archive, she governs a vast realm of stories, triumphing over the labyrinth of endless tales. Despite being the first archive conqueror, secrets and external threats persist.

Hurul
